About Us

Cetin Aslan Danismanlik is a dedicated consultancy firm assisting Turkish students in their academic journey in Italy. Our team comprises individuals who have studied and lived in Italy, offering first-hand experience and a deep understanding of the processes involved. We are committed to providing professional, reliable, and personalized services to ensure your success and well-being.

We believe in empowering students by simplifying complex procedures and offering continuous support from your initial application to settling into your new life in Italy.

Our Comprehensive Services

We offer a wide range of services tailored to meet the needs of Turkish students planning to study in Italy. Our goal is to make your transition as smooth as possible.

University Application Process Consultancy

Navigating the Italian university application system can be complex. We provide expert guidance throughout the entire process, from selecting the right course and university to preparing and submitting your application. We help you overcome challenges and ensure all requirements are met for a successful application.

Housing Support (Accommodation Consultancy)

Finding suitable accommodation in a new country is crucial. Our expert consultants assist you in finding appropriate housing options in Italy, guiding you through the rental process and ensuring you find a safe and comfortable place to live.

Scholarship Application Consultancy

We help you identify and apply for scholarships that match your profile. Our team provides guidance on preparing a strong application to maximize your chances of securing financial aid for your studies in Italy, including support for DSU and other regional scholarships.

Visa Consultancy

The visa application process can be daunting. We offer expert consultancy to reduce stress and help you prepare a complete and accurate application, increasing the likelihood of a successful outcome for your Italian student visa.

Permit of Stay Support (Oturum İzni Danışmanlığı)

We provide expert guidance on the necessary steps to obtain your permit of stay (Permesso di Soggiorno) in Italy, ensuring you comply with all legal requirements for living and studying in the country.

Airport Transfer and Welcome Service

Arrive in Italy with peace of mind. We offer reliable airport transfer and welcome services, ensuring a comfortable and stress-free start to your Italian journey. Let us handle your arrival logistics so you can focus on settling in.

Italian Language Course

Our Italian language courses are designed for students of all levels, from beginners to those looking to improve their existing skills. Learn from experienced teachers with interactive materials to quickly gain proficiency in Italian.

Rome Storage Service (Roma Depolama Hizmeti)

We offer affordable and secure storage solutions in Rome for your belongings, whether for short-term or long-term needs, including options for document archiving and seasonal storage.
